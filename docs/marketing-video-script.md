# Efficient Labs — Brand Film / Conversion Script

**Format:** 80–90s cinematic explainer · dark, chrome, electric-blue · calm authoritative VO
**Goal:** convert a technical, sovereignty-minded buyer from "interesting" → "I need this."
**Spine:** Hook → Bottlenecks (the enemy) → Turn → Solution → Why us (proof) → CTA.
**Voice:** confident, plain, a little defiant. Short sentences. No hype words, no fake stats.

---

### 1 · HOOK — "the quiet theft" (0:00–0:10)
**VO:** "Every time your AI does something brilliant… someone else owns it."
**On-screen:** *EFFICIENT LABS* wordmark flickers in, blue underline ignites.
**Visual (Shot A):** slow push toward a monolithic datacenter in the dark; thin threads of light siphon out of faceless human silhouettes and pour into it. Red warning glow underneath.

### 2 · THE BOTTLENECKS — name the enemy (0:10–0:28)
**VO:** "Today's AI is rented, not owned. Your data becomes their rent. Every token is metered. Your access can be throttled, logged, or shut off — and it burns a planet to run."
**On-screen (stagger):** RENTED · SURVEILLED · METERED · THROTTLED · EXTRACTIVE
**Visual (Shot A cont.):** the datacenter racks pulse red; a meter spins; a "burn" of heat haze rises.

### 3 · THE TURN — "but the cloud was never the sky" (0:28–0:36)
**VO:** "But the cloud was never the sky. And the sky… was always yours."
**Visual (Shot B):** the datacenter dissolves and scatters upward; the heat clears; Earth's curved atmosphere and aurora light-beams rise into frame. *(Beat. Breath.)*

### 4 · THE SOLUTION — Atmosphere + Stratos (0:36–0:58)
**VO:** "Efficient Labs builds the Atmosphere — a sovereign internet for AI agents. Your agent, StratosAgent, runs on hardware you own. It reasons locally, reaches for a frontier model only when it has to, and meshes with the world peer-to-peer — content-addressed, capability-secured, post-quantum. No landlord. No meter. No harvest."
**On-screen:** THE ATMOSPHERE · STRATOSAGENT · content-addressed / capability-secured / post-quantum
**Visual (Shot C):** a luminous peer-to-peer node mesh spreads across a dark Earth globe — no center.
**Visual (Shot D):** StratosAgent — the chrome agent reclining above Earth, typing; it looks up and waves hello. *(the wave clip)*

### 5 · WHY US — the honesty moat (0:58–1:14)
**VO:** "We automate the file architecture — not the AI wrapper. So it's cheaper, auditable, and reproducible. And we show you exactly what's real: every capability, labelled live, wired, or mock. No vaporware. Speaks MCP, bridges n8n — thousands of tools, on day one."
**On-screen:** the honest status matrix (L0→L5) scrolls; "Live / Wired / Mock" badges; integration marquee.

### 6 · CTA — the close (1:14–1:25)
**VO:** "The cloud was never yours. The sky always was. Come build in the open air."
**On-screen:** *EFFICIENT LABS* lockup + **efficientlabs.ai** + "Enter the Atmosphere →"
**Visual:** pull back from the global mesh to a single bright sovereign node — your node.

---

## Production plan
- **Generated shots (Higgsfield, seedance/kling, 16:9, dark/chrome/blue):**
  - **Shot A** — datacenter data-sharecropping (the enemy).
  - **Shot B** — cloud dissolves → sky + atmosphere reveal.
  - **Shot C** — peer-to-peer node mesh across the globe.
  - **Shot D** — StratosAgent wave (already generated; reused).
- **Screen-recorded beats:** the live site already animates §3–§5 (cloud→sky, Stratos, status matrix, marquee) — capture as b-roll so the film and the site share one visual language.
- **Assembly (post):** cut clips to the VO; add VO (ElevenLabs or human read), ambient/braam score, kinetic captions in Michroma/JetBrains Mono. This final edit is the one step outside the generate-pipeline — deliverable is the script + graded shots + b-roll + an edit list.
